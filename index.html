About Us


CalTex was formed in El Paso, Texas in 2016 with the vision of taking cooking to a different level by introducing unique flavors of barbeque sauce. The first flavor we created was Original, followed by Del's Favorite. Next came our Drunken Cherry, Apple Sensation and several other tasty sauces. We take great pride in our sauce and we strive for perfection because we know that's what our customers expect.


We also offer delicious BBQ meals. These include your choice of ribs, chicken or sausage smothered in our homemade BBQ sauce. All you have to do is pre-order your meal and we will deliver it right to your door.


We are a company made by veterans for our veterans. We offer a 15% discount to military personnel and veterans and 10% of all proceeds are donated to a veterans organization. Thank you for your service.
